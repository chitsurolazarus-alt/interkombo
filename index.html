About Us

Crafting Excellence in Every Print

Who We Are

Inter Kombo stands as a premier printing and branding establishment, meticulously engineered to elevate corporate identities through sophisticated visual communications.

Our Vision

To establish ourselves as the preeminent regional branding and promotional enterprise, distinguished by pioneering innovation and uncompromising production excellence.

Our Mission

To provide seamlessly integrated, cost-effective, and superior quality branding solutions that enable organizations to articulate their identity with clarity.

Our Capabilities

Comprehensive In-House Solutions

Screen Printing

Premium quality screen printing for bulk orders with vibrant, long-lasting results

Embroidery

Professional embroidery on apparel and accessories with intricate detail and precision

UV Printing

UV-resistant printing technology for exceptional durability and stunning color reproduction

Pad Printing

Precision pad printing on irregular surfaces with exceptional accuracy and consistency

Large Format Printing

Large scale printing for impactful banners, displays, and promotional materials

Vinyl & Dome Stickers

Durable vinyl and dome stickers with weatherproof properties for lasting impressions

Laser Engraving

Precision laser engraving services for permanent, elegant markings on various materials

Heat Press

Professional heat press applications for flawless transfers on various substrates

Sublimation

High-quality sublimation printing for vibrant, permanent color integration into materials

DTF Printing

Direct-to-film printing technology for versatile, high-quality transfers on any fabric

And more: Signage & Banners, Corporate Gifts, Vehicle Branding, Promotional Products
